# Managing Up

**Scope:** The relationship, narrative, and perception upward — with your direct manager and, where relevant, skip-levels and dotted-line execs. Tracks stakeholder profiles (observable behavior only), 1:1 prep and capture, executive summaries translating team work to higher altitude, and the cadence of touchpoints that shape perception.

**Out of scope:** Direct reports (Managing Down); peers and cross-functional relationships (Managing Sideways); external peer CTOs or advisors (External Network & Thought Leadership); board-level communication (Board Comms); organization-wide comms (Org Comms).

**Frameworks:** [Andy Grove — *High Output Management*](https://www.amazon.com/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884), stakeholder-needs analysis (cross-cutting profile format).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none
- Optional: `personal-os` (voice, show-up, stated intent), `business-alignment` (what's worth translating upward), `team-management` (source material for exec summaries), `attention-operations` (flags boss-originated items)

**Example tasks:**
- "Prep for my 1:1 with my boss tomorrow."
- "Log today's 1:1 — topics, decisions, follow-ups, and what I noticed."
- "Draft an exec summary of this quarter's platform work for my boss."
- "Help me sell the reorg proposal upward before I pitch it formally."
- "My boss pushed back on the hiring ask — help me reframe."

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/managing-up/state/`

**Sensitivity:** high (candid assessments of upward stakeholders, 1:1 content).
