# Attention & Operations

**Scope:** Daily and weekly operational rhythm plus inbox triage. Filters incoming signal across Slack, email, Linear, and PRs to separate attention-needed from FYI. Produces three recurring artifacts: morning briefing, Monday week-starter, Friday week-wrap.

**Out of scope:** Personal reflection / goals / show-up (Personal OS); team-aggregate operations (Team Management); incident response (Tech Ops); individual performance tracking (Performance & Development).

**Frameworks:** [GTD — David Allen](https://gettingthingsdone.com/), [Eisenhower Matrix](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_management#The_Eisenhower_Method).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none
- Optional: `personal-os` (stated intent re-ranks triage priority)

**Example tasks:**
- "Morning briefing — what happened overnight and what's on for today."
- "Week-starter: what's on for this week, plus carry-over from Friday."
- "Week-wrap: what shipped this week."
- "Triage my Slack and email — surface anything that needs a response."
- "Recruiter emails should now be attention-needed — I started a search."

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/attention-operations/state/`
