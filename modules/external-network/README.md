# External Network & Thought Leadership

**Scope:** External relationships (peer CTOs, advisors, mentors, mentees, industry peers) and public presence as a senior engineering leader (conference speaking, public writing, podcasts, community engagement). Tracks contacts, touchpoints, commitments through lifecycle, delivered artifacts, and overall public-presence posture.

**Out of scope:** Internal peer relationships (Managing Sideways); internal thought leadership (Org Comms); upward / downward internal relationships (Managing Up / Down); technical substance of what's being written or presented (Technical Strategy owns content; this module tracks presence).

**Frameworks:** [Mark Granovetter — *The Strength of Weak Ties*](https://www.jstor.org/stable/2776392), [Dorie Clark — *Stand Out*](https://dorieclark.com/standout/).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none
- Optional: `personal-os` (voice and show-up for public artifacts), `attention-operations` (flags external items needing response)

**Example tasks:**
- "Add Jane (peer CTO at AcmeCo) as an external contact."
- "Log coffee with Mark — we talked about platform strategy and his new role."
- "Submitted to QCon — log the commitment."
- "Update my public posture — I'm going low-profile for six months."
- "Show me who I haven't talked to in six months."

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/external-network/state/`
