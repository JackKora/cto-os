# Business Alignment

**Scope:** The connection between what engineering does and what the business needs. Tracks company goals, captures external signal from customer-facing teams (sales, marketing, support, onboarding), logs CTO-level customer engagements, and makes work-to-goals ties visible. Foundational — consumed by Board Comms, Org Comms, Org Design, and others.

**Out of scope:** Personal goals and cascade (Personal OS); team-level goals (Team Management); individual performance (Performance & Development); board-level strategic communication (Board Comms); internal comms reporting on goals progress (Org Comms).

**Frameworks:** [Colin Bryar & Bill Carr — *Working Backwards*](https://www.amazon.com/Working-Backwards-Insights-Stories-Secrets/dp/1250267595), [Clayton Christensen — Jobs to be Done](https://hbr.org/2016/09/know-your-customers-jobs-to-be-done).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none (foundational)
- Optional: none

**Example tasks:**
- "Capture Q2 company goals and tie them to our engineering priorities."
- "I had a customer advisory call — log the signal."
- "Show me the work-to-goals heatmap — any company goals with no initiative against them?"
- "Sales keeps hearing the same complaint — capture it."
- "Draft the engineering update for the CEO's all-hands off the current work-mapping."
