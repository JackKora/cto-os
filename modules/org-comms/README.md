# Organizational Communications

**Scope:** Communicating to internal audiences at scale — the voice of leadership through recurring and ad-hoc updates. Composes content by pulling from Team Management, Business Alignment, Process Management, and Tech Ops. Tracks declared surfaces (channels / lists / wikis), recurring cadences (monthly eng update, quarterly all-hands), and delivered comms (what went out, for voice consistency and longitudinal reference). Strategic and periodic module.

**Out of scope:** Board-level communication (Board Comms); external audiences and thought leadership (External Network & Thought Leadership); single-team comms to your direct reports (Managing Down); 1:1 or small-group comms (Managing Up / Down / Sideways).

**Frameworks:** [Amazon narrative format / 6-pager](https://www.amazon.com/Working-Backwards-Insights-Stories-Secrets/dp/1250267595), [Barbara Minto — *The Pyramid Principle*](https://www.amazon.com/Pyramid-Principle-Logic-Writing-Thinking/dp/0273710516).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none
- Optional: `personal-os` (voice, show-up, tone), `business-alignment` (goals progress for updates), `team-management` (team state), `tech-ops` (reliability narrative, incident comms), `process-management` (flow KPIs). Falls back to generic professional voice when Personal OS is inactive.

**Example tasks:**
- "Draft the monthly engineering update."
- "Write the eng section for next week's all-hands."
- "Draft internal incident comms for yesterday's outage."
- "Announce the reorg to the whole eng org."
- "What comms do I owe this month?"

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/org-comms/state/`
