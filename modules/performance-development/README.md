# Performance & Development

**Scope:** Developing the people the user has hired, over the arc of their careers. Review cycles, leveling ladder reference, per-person performance records tracking trends, development plans with goals and check-ins, calibration sessions, promotion-case authorship, PIP lifecycle, succession-planning notes for key roles. Strategic and periodic module — runs in cycles, not daily.

**Out of scope:** Pre-ramp hiring and initial ramp (Hiring); team-aggregate health (Team Management — but this module's individual trends roll up there); day-to-day 1:1 coaching (Managing Down — those are *input* here); peer or upward performance (not the user's to manage).

**Frameworks:** [Kim Scott — *Radical Candor*](https://www.radicalcandor.com/), [Carol Dweck — *Mindset* (growth mindset)](https://www.amazon.com/Mindset-Psychology-Carol-S-Dweck/dp/0345472322).

**Depends on:**
- Required: `team-management`
- Optional: `hiring` (ramp-plan handoff at end of ramp), `managing-down` (coaching signals as review input)

**Example tasks:**
- "Draft a performance review for Jane — cycle input is here."
- "Prep for calibration — where does Mike sit relative to staff?"
- "Build a promotion case for Alex to senior staff."
- "Open a PIP for [report] — here's the gap and success criteria."
- "Succession planning for the VP of Platform role — who's ready?"

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/performance-development/state/`

**Sensitivity:** high (career-impacting reviews, PIPs with legal implications, calibration discussions, promotion cases).
