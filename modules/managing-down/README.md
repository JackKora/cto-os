# Managing Down

**Scope:** Leading and developing the people who report to the user directly. The leadership relationship — 1:1s, coaching moments outside 1:1s, delegation, feedback in the moment, team-wide communication, building and maintaining trust. Tracks observable profiles of reports and the cadence of interactions that accrue between 1:1s.

**Out of scope:** Individual performance tracking, calibration, promotions, PIPs (Performance & Development); team-aggregate health (Team Management); peer relationships (Managing Sideways); upward relationships (Managing Up); pre-ramp hiring (Hiring).

**Frameworks:** [Kim Scott — *Radical Candor*](https://www.radicalcandor.com/), [Andy Grove — *High Output Management*](https://www.amazon.com/High-Output-Management-Andrew-Grove/dp/0679762884).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none
- Optional: `team-management` (who your reports are, team-level context), `performance-development` (development threads feed 1:1 signal), `personal-os` (voice, show-up), `attention-operations`

**Example tasks:**
- "Prep for my 1:1 with Jane tomorrow."
- "Log today's 1:1 with Mike — topics, decisions, follow-ups, and what I noticed."
- "I just gave [report] feedback on yesterday's review — log it as a coaching event."
- "Draft team-wide comms announcing the reorg to my team."
- "Mike has been drifting — help me think through how to re-engage him."

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/managing-down/state/`

**Sensitivity:** high (1:1 content, coaching notes, candid observations of reports).
