# Board Comms

**Scope:** Board-level strategic communication. Composes quarterly board updates and topic-specific pre-reads (risk briefings, fundraising narratives, M&A analysis) by pulling from Business Alignment, Process Management, Tech Ops, Security & Compliance, Hiring, and Budget. Captures what happens at the meeting itself — director feedback, decisions, follow-ups — so narrative continuity holds across quarters. Strategic and periodic module.

**Out of scope:** Operational internal comms (Org Comms); external investor or peer relationships outside board meetings (External Network & Thought Leadership); all-hands content for broader audiences (Org Comms); day-to-day exec comms (Managing Up or Managing Sideways).

**Frameworks:** [Brad Feld & Mahendra Ramsinghani — *Startup Boards*](https://feld.com/category/books/startup-boards/), [Amazon narrative format / 6-pager](https://www.amazon.com/Working-Backwards-Insights-Stories-Secrets/dp/1250267595).

**Depends on:**
- Required: `business-alignment`, `process-management`
- Optional: `tech-ops`, `security-compliance`, `hiring`, `budget`, `personal-os`

**Example tasks:**
- "Draft the CTO section for the Q2 board update."
- "Write the pre-read on the Kafka migration for next board."
- "Log yesterday's board meeting — here's what directors pushed on."
- "Risk briefing for the board on the SOC 2 audit findings."
- "Update board structure — new lead-independent director joined."

**State location:** `cto-os-data/modules/board-comms/state/`

**Sensitivity:** high (material risks, financial posture, strategic and personnel moves at the exec level).
