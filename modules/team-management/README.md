# Team Management

**Scope:** The ongoing health and performance of the teams in your org, at the team-aggregate level. How each team is doing against a standardized rubric, how it's staffed, how it's evolving over time. Daily driver — operational hub that Performance & Development and Managing Down consume for roll-up views.

**Out of scope:** Individual performance (Performance & Development); 1:1 coaching and direct-report leadership (Managing Down); strategic team structure changes (Org Design makes the decisions; this module tracks the current reality); personal retros (Personal OS); hiring pipeline execution (Hiring).

**Frameworks:** [Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais — *Team Topologies*](https://teamtopologies.com/), [Patrick Lencioni — *The Five Dysfunctions of a Team*](https://www.tablegroup.com/product/dysfunctions/).

**Depends on:**
- Required: none
- Optional: `hiring` (incoming ramps), `performance-development` (individual trends roll up to team view)

**Example tasks:**
- "Score the platform team on our rubric."
- "Log a team retro for growth squad — they wrapped their sprint."
- "Show me team health across the org — which teams are trending down?"
- "Add a new team: infra-security, led by Jane, 4 people, stream-aligned."
- "Deprecate the legacy-search team — it's being absorbed into platform."
